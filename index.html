Live Without Limitations

Live Without Limitations is an interactive assembly program started by Gabe Hurley, who knows first hand how your life can change in an instant. After a horrific crash and twelve surgeries, resulting in permanent blindness, Gabe began to leverage his experience into a motivating and inspirational story. For as much as Hurley lost, there was something gained. One of his hands was broken but not enough to hamper his guitar playing. He continued playing with his band, The New Black, like before. He now shares his struggle, inner strength, and triumph with mesmerized audiences around New Jersey.

Incorporating his love of music and his talent as a guitar player, Gabe works to help teenagers understand the importance of their choices, and how one small choice can change someone else's life forever.

Music

Music is a big part of Gabe Hurley's presentations. Why music? Students of all ages, backgrounds and personalities love music. Connecting a message of safe driving and healthy choices shared through music will be an effective and practical way to make a difference.
